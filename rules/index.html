SERVER RULES

Keep the community fun, fair, and enjoyable for everyone.

01

No Cheating

Hacks, x-ray, auto clickers, dupes, and unfair advantages are strictly prohibited.

02

Respect Everyone

Toxicity, harassment, hate speech, and personal attacks are not tolerated.

03

No Griefing

Destroying builds, stealing items, or intentionally ruining player progress is forbidden.

04

No Lag Machines

Any build or mechanism designed to intentionally lag the server will be removed.

05

Use Common Sense

If something negatively affects the community or server experience, don't do it.
