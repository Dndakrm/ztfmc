OUR STORY

ZEFTY was created by two friends known as ZEDD and FROSTY. The name came from combining their aliases: ZEdd + FrosTY.

What started as a small private SMP in 2024 slowly turned into a growing Minecraft community. After a year-long hiatus, the two creators are finally back.
