OUR STORY

Built by two friends. Powered by community. Survived through time.

THE BEGINNING

ZEFTY MC started back in 2024 by two friends known as ZEDD and FROSTY.

The name ZEFTY came from combining their aliases: ZEdd + FrosTY.

Originally, the server was never meant to become a public community. It was simply a small SMP made for fun — just two people wanting a place to survive, build, and spend time together in Minecraft.

But over time, things slowly grew bigger. More players joined, more memories were made, and what began as a private survival world transformed into an actual community.

COMMUNITY ERA

The server slowly evolved into a growing public survival community with players from different places joining together.

THE RETURN

After nearly a year of hiatus, the original creators returned to rebuild ZEFTY MC once again.

MORE THAN A SERVER

ZEFTY MC was never built to become a massive corporate Minecraft network.

It's a place made by players who simply love Minecraft and want to create a fun, welcoming, and memorable experience for others.

Even after all this time, the same two creators are still here, continuing the story they started together years ago.
